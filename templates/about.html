About Bolakin Educational Consult

Founded with a vision to provide seamless educational consulting services, Bolakin Educational Consult has helped thousands of students achieve their dream of studying abroad at prestigious institutions worldwide.

Our Story

Bolakin Educational Consult was established in 2015 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between aspiring students and quality international education. What started as a small consulting office has now grown into a trusted name in educational consulting services.

Our founder, Mrs. Bolakin, envisioned a service that would provide personalized guidance to students navigating the complex process of studying abroad. With her experience in international education and a passion for helping students, she built a team of dedicated professionals who share her vision.

Today, we pride ourselves on our track record of successful placements in top universities across the globe, and our commitment to excellence remains as strong as ever.

Our Mission & Values

At Bolakin Educational Consult, our mission is to empower students to achieve their academic and career goals through quality international education. We are guided by the following core values:

  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service.
  • Student-Centered: We place the needs and aspirations of our students at the center of everything we do.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek innovative solutions to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
  • Diversity: We embrace and celebrate diversity in all its forms.

These values have shaped our approach and have been instrumental in our success story.

What We Offer

We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to simplify the process of studying abroad:

  • Educational Counseling: Personalized guidance to help students choose the right course and institution based on their academic background, career goals, and budget.
  • Application Processing: Assistance with preparing and submitting applications to international universities.
  • Visa Guidance: Expert advice and support for visa application processes.
  • Scholarship Assistance: Help in identifying and applying for scholarship opportunities.
  • Pre-Departure Orientation: Comprehensive briefing to prepare students for life abroad.
  • Accommodation Assistance: Support in finding suitable accommodation options.

Our holistic approach ensures that students are well-prepared for their international education journey.
