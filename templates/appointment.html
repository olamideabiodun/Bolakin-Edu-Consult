Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our consultation services.

How long does the consultation process take?
Our standard consultation typically lasts 45-60 minutes. However, we understand that each student's needs are unique, and we're flexible to extend the session if needed to ensure all your questions are addressed.
Is there a fee for the initial consultation?
We offer a complimentary initial consultation to understand your needs and provide general guidance. Subsequent specialized consultations or service packages may have associated fees, which will be clearly communicated beforehand.
What should I prepare for the consultation?
To make the most of your consultation, please have your academic transcripts, certificates, CV/resume, passport, and any standardized test scores (if available) ready. Also, prepare questions and be clear about your academic and career goals.
Can I change my appointment time after booking?
Yes, you can reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Please contact our office via email or phone to request a change in your appointment time.
Do you offer virtual consultations?
Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual consultations via Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp video call. Virtual consultations provide the same level of personalized guidance and are especially convenient for international clients.
What happens after the consultation?
After the consultation, we'll provide you with a summary of our discussion and recommended next steps. If you decide to proceed with our services, we'll outline a personalized plan and guide you through each stage of the application process.
